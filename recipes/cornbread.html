Amusement Park Cornbread

Description

This is cornbread reserved only for royalty! First introduced in the Magic Kingdom the recipe has since been stolen and now able to be enjoyed by all of us common folk. If you are wondering if there are any moral problems with enjoying a recipe that was stolen I am sorry I cannot help you. I am simply here to give you the information of the recipe and nothing else.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 3/4 cup cornmeal
  • 1 1/3 cups milk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease an 8 inch skillet.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together sugar, salt, butter and vanilla until creamy. Stir in eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. In a separate bowl, mix together flour, baking powder and cornmeal. Stir flour mixture into egg mixture alternately with the milk. Beat well until blended.
  3. Bake in preheated oven for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Serve warm.
